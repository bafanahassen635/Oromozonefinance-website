Dear esteemed clients of the Oromo especial Administration zone Finance Department, we would like to extend our respectful greetings to you. We would like to convey my message that our institution is effectively carrying out the responsibilities entrusted to it in order to ensure the success of the multifaceted development works undertaken by our department. As it is known, the development activities carried out at any level in our department can only be successful when we use our limited financial and material resources properly and transparently, and when we manage our financial administration system in a modern way. In order to manage money and property in a modern way, it is crucial to systematically guide existing practices with the help of technology. We would like to take this opportunity to state that our finance sector department is working diligently to transition towards sustainability.
