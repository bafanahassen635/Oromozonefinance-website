Brief Information on the Oromo Special Administration Zone The Oromo Special Administration Zone is one of the three Special zones in the Amara Region. This zone is a special zone primarily located between the South Wollo and North Shewa zones, as well as along the border line of the Afar Region. Its climate makes it suitable for various crop productions (such as sorghum, teff, and maize) as well as for livestock rearing. Its total area is estimated to be approximately between 4,100 and 4,300 square kilometers. Its topography includes vast lowland plains, subtropical (Weyna Dega) hills, and a few mountainous areas.
